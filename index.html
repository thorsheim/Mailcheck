DNS Email Security Checker

Check DNSSEC, MX, SPF, DKIM and more for any domain

Type in a domain name like google.com or microsoft.com, and click Check. Please understand that results may not be correct due to limitations in your browser or in the network from which you are testing. Scores are for indicative purposes only, they do not necessarily tell the full truth or show compliance with relevant standards.
